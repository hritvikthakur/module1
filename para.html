Masters in Digital Marketing in the USA is a one to two-year-long program
available as MSc, MA and MBA specialization. The tuition fee for pursuing a master's degree in digital marketing in US universities ranges from 23,000 to 76,000 USD. Indian students planning to pursue the course will need an equivalent amount of around 17 to 56 lakh INR .
